- 4-quadrant ROI framework (Cost, Time, Quality, Retention)

## 🏗️ SECTION 82.1: HIRING ROI QUADRANT FRAMEWORK

### A. Quadrant 1: Cost-Per-Hire Optimization (CPH)

**Definition:** Total cost to fill one position

**Calculation:**
```
CPH = (Total_Recruiting_Spend / Total_Hires) 

Where Total_Recruiting_Spend includes:
  - Tool subscriptions (ATS, sourcing, job board)
  - Recruiter salaries (prorated)
  - Interview platform costs
  - Hiring manager time (prorated)
  - Background checks + onboarding
  - Employer branding / advertising
  - Agency fees (if used)

Industry Baseline: $4,000-8,000 per hire
EcoSkiller Target: <$1,500 per hire (-75% vs. industry)
```

**Cost Breakdown by Source:**

```
Traditional Hiring (Industry):
├─ Job Posting: $500 (board fees)
├─ Sourcing: $800 (recruiter time)
├─ Screening: $600 (recruiter interviews)
├─ Interviewing: $400 (manager time)
├─ Tools: $300 (ATS, video)
├─ Background Checks: $200
├─ Agency Fees: $2,000 (if used)
└─ Total: $4,800 per hire

EcoSkiller Optimized Hiring:
├─ Job Posting: $0 (integrated)
├─ Sourcing: $200 (AI matching from Dojo)
├─ Screening: $400 (skill verification, self-assessment)
├─ Interviewing: $600 (standardized, Dojo-based)
├─ Tools: $50 (included in platform)
├─ Background Checks: $150
├─ Platform Fee: $100 (split with employer)
└─ Total: $1,500 per hire

Savings: $3,300 per hire (69% reduction)
```

**Cost Attribution Model:**

```

  Cost: $200/hire

  Speed: 8 days to hire

### B. Quadrant 2: Time-To-Fill Acceleration (TTF)

**Definition:** Days from job open to offer acceptance

**Calculation:**
```
TTF = Days_Posted to Days_Offer_Accepted

Industry Baseline: 42 days
EcoSkiller Target: <30 days (-29% vs. industry)

Breakdown:
  Stage 1 (Sourcing & Screening): Target <5 days
  Stage 2 (Interviewing): Target <10 days
  Stage 3 (Offer & Negotiation): Target <5 days
  Stage 4 (Background & Start): Target <10 days
  Total: 30 days
```

**TTF by Hiring Path:**

```
Path A: Direct Dojo Sourcing (FASTEST)
├─ Day 1: Post job + AI matching (2 hours)
├─ Day 2: Pre-screen: Automated skill test (candidates self-take)
├─ Day 3: Candidate ranks by proven skills + Dojo belt
├─ Day 4: Interview (1x 30-min with hiring manager)
├─ Day 5: Decision made (clear signal from Dojo proof)
├─ Day 6: Offer made
├─ Day 8: Background + onboarding starts
└─ TTF: 8 days ✅

Path B: LinkedIn + Manual Screening (MODERATE)
├─ Day 1-3: Post job, recruit on LinkedIn
├─ Day 4-7: Resume review, outreach (multiple candidates)
├─ Day 8-12: Screening calls (multiple rounds)
├─ Day 13-18: Technical interviews (multiple rounds)
├─ Day 19-22: Team interviews (multiple rounds)
├─ Day 23: Decision made
├─ Day 25: Offer made
├─ Day 30: Start date
└─ TTF: 30 days ✅

Path C: Agency Recruiting (SLOW)
├─ Day 1-3: Brief agent, define role
├─ Day 4-14: Agent sources candidates
├─ Day 15-20: Present candidates (often weak candidates)
├─ Day 21-30: Interview + decision (if candidate quality allows)
├─ Day 31-35: Offer negotiation (with agent overhead)
├─ Day 36-40: Background + legal
├─ Day 41-45: Start date
└─ TTF: 45 days ✗ (industry average, slow)
```

**TTF Improvement Levers:**

```
Lever 1: AI-Powered Matching (Saves 7 days)
  Problem: Manual sourcing = slow (recruiters review resumes)
  Solution: AI matches job to Dojo skill profile (instant)
  Impact: Candidates pre-qualified by skills, not resume words
  Savings: 7 days (from 14 days → 7 days sourcing/screening)

Lever 2: Skill Verification (Saves 5 days)
  Problem: Interviews validate claims (multiple rounds)
  Solution: Dojo belt + match replays = proof upfront
  Impact: 1 interview instead of 3-4 (clear capability proof)
  Savings: 5 days (from 15 days → 10 days interviewing)

Lever 3: Standardized Interviews (Saves 3 days)
  Problem: Scheduling back-and-forth = coordination overhead
  Solution: 1x 30-min interview, async scoring
  Impact: Same-day scheduling possible
  Savings: 3 days (from 5 days → 2 days coordination)

Lever 4: Offer Automation (Saves 2 days)
  Problem: Manual offer generation, legal review
  Solution: Template-based offers, auto-generated
  Impact: Same-day offer dispatch
  Savings: 2 days (from 3 days → 1 day offer)

Total Savings: 17 days (45 days → 28 days)
```

### C. Quadrant 3: Quality-Of-Hire Measurement (QOH)

**Definition:** Job performance match within first 6 months

**Calculation:**
```
QOH = (Retained_After_6mo / Total_Hired) × 100 + Job_Performance_Score

Industry Baseline: 
  - Retention: 75% (25% leave within 6 months)
  - Performance: 65% meeting expectations
  - Combined: ~70% "good hires"

EcoSkiller Target: 
  - Retention: >90% (only 10% leave)
  - Performance: >85% meeting expectations
  - Combined: >87% "high-quality hires"
```

```

  Measurement:
    - Day 30: Onboarding satisfaction (survey)

  Signals of poor QOH:
    ✗ Leaves within 30 days (onboarding mismatch)
    ✗ Leaves within 90 days (role mismatch)
    ✗ Leaves within 180 days (cultural fit issue)

Dimension 2: Job Performance (After 6 months)

    Sales Role:

### D. Quadrant 4: Hiring Pipeline ROI Composite (HPI)

**Definition:** Unified ROI metric accounting for all hiring dimensions

**Calculation:**
```
HPI = (CPH_Reduction% × 0.25) + (TTF_Speed% × 0.25) + (QOH_Score × 0.3) + (Retention% × 0.2)

Where:
  CPH_Reduction% = (Industry_CPH - Your_CPH) / Industry_CPH × 100
  TTF_Speed% = (Industry_TTF - Your_TTF) / Industry_TTF × 100
  QOH_Score = (Retained_% × 0.5) + (Performance_% × 0.5)
  Retention% = Employees_still_employed_after_6mo / Total_hired × 100

Industry Baseline HPI: 65 (industry standard)
EcoSkiller Target HPI: >85 (exceeds standard)
```

**HPI Calculation Example (Enterprise with 50 hires/quarter):**

```
Metrics:
  CPH: $1,800 (vs. industry $4,500)
    CPH_Reduction% = (4500-1800)/4500 × 100 = 60%
  
  TTF: 26 days (vs. industry 42 days)
    TTF_Speed% = (42-26)/42 × 100 = 38%
  
  QOH: 87% (vs. industry 70%)
    QOH_Score = (91% retained × 0.5) + (83% performance × 0.5) = 87
  
  Retention: 91% at 6 months
    Retention% = 91

HPI = (60 × 0.25) + (38 × 0.25) + (87 × 0.3) + (91 × 0.2)
    = 15 + 9.5 + 26.1 + 18.2
    = 68.8

Status: BELOW TARGET (target >85)
Action: Focus on TTF (only 38% improvement vs. target 60%)
```

## 💡 SECTION 82.3: ANTIGRAVITY HIRING OPTIMIZATION

### A. Friction-Free Hiring Workflows

**Traditional Hiring Friction (Industry Standard):**
```
Problem 1: Resume-Based Screening (Slow + Inaccurate)
  Issue: Recruiter reads resumes, misses qualified candidates
  Time: 3-5 days per candidate
  Accuracy: 45% false negatives (good candidates passed over)
  
Problem 2: Multiple Interview Rounds (Exhausting)
  Issue: Different interviewers ask same questions
  Time: 3-4 rounds, 5-10 hours candidate time
  Accuracy: Low consistency in evaluation
  
Problem 3: Manual Offer & Negotiation (Tedious)
  Issue: Back-and-forth on terms, legal review delays
  Time: 3-5 days per offer
  Accuracy: High error rate, slow closing
  
Problem 4: Onboarding Chaos (Unstructured)
  Issue: No clear first-day plan, unclear role expectations
  Time: 30 days to productivity (slow ramp)
  Accuracy: 25% leave within 6 months (poor fit)
```

**EcoSkiller Antigravity Solution:**

**Lever 1: Skill-Based Matching (Replace Resume Screening)**
```
Old Way (Resume):
  "Senior Python Developer, 5 years experience"
  → Recruiter skims resume, might be good or terrible
  
New Way (Dojo Skill Proof):
  Candidate has "Blue Belt" in Python (Dojo certified)
  → Verified through live match performance + score
  → Proof is in match replays (visible to hiring manager)
  → Zero ambiguity (either has skill or doesn't)

Impact:
  - Screening time: 5 days → 2 hours
  - Screening accuracy: 45% → 92%
  - False negatives: 45% eliminated → 8%
  - Hiring speed: +3 days saved
```

**Lever 2: One-Interview Model (Replace Multi-Round)**
```
Old Way (Multiple Rounds):
  Round 1: Phone screen (recruiter)
  Round 2: Technical screen (engineer)
  Round 3: Team interview (manager)
  Round 4: Executive interview (director)
  → 4 rounds × 1-2 weeks = 4-8 weeks total
  → Candidate fatigued, good candidates drop out

New Way (Dojo-Informed Single Interview):
  Pre-interview: Dojo skill matches + evidence (30 min review)
  Interview: 30-min structured conversation (manager + h-skip)
  Post-interview: Scoring against Dojo skill map
  → 1 round × 1-2 days = 2-3 days total
  → Candidate fresh, high offer acceptance rate

Impact:
  - Interview load: 4 rounds → 1 round
  - Interview time (hiring manager): 10 hours → 2 hours
  - Candidate fatigue: High → Low
  - Offer acceptance: 75% → 92%
  - TTF: 35 days → 20 days (-43%)
```

**Lever 3: Automated Offer Generation (Replace Manual)**
```
Old Way (Manual Offer):
  Step 1: Recruiter drafts offer (2 hours)
  Step 2: Manager reviews + approves (1 hour)
  Step 3: HR legal review (8 hours)
  Step 4: Candidate negotiation (3 days)
  → Total: 4 days + 11 hours
  → Candidate anxiety: "When do I get the offer?"

New Way (Templated + Automated):
  Step 1: System generates offer from template (2 min)
  Step 2: Manager reviews + e-signs (10 min)
  Step 3: Candidate e-signs (5 min)
  → Total: 1 day (same-day offer)
  → Candidate delight: Instant gratification

Impact:
  - Offer generation: 2 hours → 2 min
  - Legal review: 8 hours → included in system
  - Negotiation cycle: 3 days → 0 days (take-it-or-leave-it template)
  - Offer-to-start: 14 days → 7 days
  - Offer acceptance: 85% → 96%
  - TTF: -7 days saved
```

**Lever 4: Structured Onboarding (Replace Ad-Hoc)**
```
Old Way (Unstructured Onboarding):
  Day 1: "Here's your desk. Good luck."
  Week 1: Figure out where things are, who to talk to
  Week 2-4: Slowly ramping up
  Month 2-6: Still not fully productive
  → 6 months to full productivity
  → 25% quit in first 6 months (poor fit realized)

New Way (Structured + Dojo-Aligned):
  Day 1: Welcome kit + role video + first task assigned
  Week 1: Daily standups, buddy check-ins, clear milestones
  Week 2-4: Graduated responsibility (small → medium → large tasks)
  Week 5-8: Independent contributor (onboarding complete)
  → 30 days to productivity (initial)
  → 90 days to full mastery
  → <10% quit (good fit, clear expectations)

Impact:
  - Ramp-up time: 6 months → 30 days (80% faster)
  - Time-to-value: Month 3 → Week 2
  - Early retention: 75% → 94%
  - Satisfaction: 3.5/5 → 4.5/5
  - Hiring success: 70% → 90%
```
