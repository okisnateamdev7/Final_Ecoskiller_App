## 📋 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Corporate Benchmark Model (CBM) is the **unified competitive intelligence system** that contextualizes organizational performance across 4 dimensions:

1. **INDUSTRY BENCHMARKING** (How you compare to industry averages)
2. **PEER COMPARISON** (How you compare to similar companies)
3. **SKILL-BASED BENCHMARKING** (How your team's skills compare)
4. **TREND ANALYSIS** (Are you improving or declining vs. competitors?)

**Core Architecture:**
- 12-dimension benchmark framework (across Productivity, Hiring, Retention)
- Real-time industry data aggregation (100+ SaaS companies)
- Anonymized peer comparison (transparency without exposing competitor data)
- Skill-based talent quality measurement (Dojo belt distribution)
- Antigravity mechanics (transparency, fair comparison, actionable insights)

**Key Benchmark Metrics:**
- **Organizational Health Index (OHI):** Composite score across all dimensions
- **Talent Quality Index (TQI):** Skill-based team capability measurement
- **Operational Efficiency Index (OEI):** Productivity vs. peer standards
- **Talent ROI Index (TRI):** Hiring + retention + productivity combined

**Financial Impact:** +$500K-$2M annually (from data-driven optimization)

## 🔐 SECTION 83.6: SEALED GOVERNANCE

### A. Immutable Benchmarking Rules

**Rule 1: Data Privacy (Peer Data Protected)**
```
Individual company data NEVER disclosed.
Only aggregated benchmarks shared.
Peer comparison anonymized (Company A vs. Company B forbidden).
Percentile ranking shown without source identification.

Violation: Data privacy audit + remediation + user notification
```

**Rule 2: Statistical Validity**
```
Benchmarks only shown if:
  - Minimum 10 data points in cohort (statistical significance)
  - 12-month history (seasonal variation controlled)
  - Methodology transparent (clear how calculated)
  - Confidence interval disclosed

Violation: Benchmark suppressed until valid, user notified
```

**Rule 3: Fair Comparison**
```
Comparisons only within appropriate cohorts:
  - Industry (SaaS vs. Professional Services vs. Manufacturing)
  - Company size (50-200 emps vs. 200-500 vs. 500+ emps)
  - Geography (if applicable)
  - Role type (engineering vs. sales vs. CS)

NO cross-industry comparisons (SaaS vs. Manufacturing invalid).
NO cross-size comparisons (startup vs. enterprise invalid).

Violation: Benchmark removed, fair cohort grouping enforced
```

**Rule 4: Actionable Insights**
```
Every benchmark includes:
  ✓ Your score
  ✓ Industry average
  ✓ Your percentile
  ✓ Path to improvement (specific actions)
  ✓ Expected impact ($, time, %)

NOT just a ranking ("You're in 80th percentile")
BUT guidance ("Here's how to reach 90th percentile")

Violation: Benchmark redesigned for actionability
```

**Rule 5: No Competitive Harm**
```
Benchmarking information used for:
  ✓ Self-improvement (beating your own metrics)
  ✓ Industry understanding (knowing standards)
  ✓ Best practice adoption (learning from leaders)

NOT used for:
  ✗ Predatory pricing (undercutting based on competitor data)
  ✗ Talent poaching (targeting specific companies)
  ✗ Market share warfare
  ✗ Confidential strategy disclosure

Enforcement: Terms of service, user agreement, auditing

Violation: Account suspension, legal action if required
```
