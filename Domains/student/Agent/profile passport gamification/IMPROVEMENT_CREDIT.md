  improvement arc toward the standard.

IMPROVEMENT_CREDIT_AGENT produces the **growth dimension of intelligence**

## 4️⃣ IMPROVEMENT CREDIT COMPUTATION MODEL

### 4.1 — Raw Delta Calculation

```
For every performance signal, the raw delta is computed as:

  raw_delta = current_value - baseline_value

  where:
    current_value = score/metric in current measurement window
    baseline_value = rolling average of same metric over prior 30 days

  Baseline is PERSONAL — not cohort-relative at this stage.
  Positive delta = improvement candidate
  Zero delta = no change → credit = 0
  Negative delta = regression → no credit, regression flag

WINDOW DEFINITIONS:
  SHORT_WINDOW  = 7 days   (for streak and consistency signals)
  MEDIUM_WINDOW = 30 days  (primary performance signals)
  LONG_WINDOW   = 90 days  (for stagnation and decay detection)
```

### 4.2 — Quality-Adjusted Delta (Anti-Inflation)

```
Raw deltas are quality-adjusted before credit conversion:

  adjusted_delta = raw_delta × difficulty_weight × consistency_weight
                   × integrity_weight × novelty_weight

  WHERE:

  difficulty_weight:
    Scenario/content difficulty (from community difficulty rating + tier):
    EASY tier:     0.60
    MEDIUM tier:   0.85
    HARD tier:     1.00
    ELITE tier:    1.25
    UNKNOWN:       0.70 (conservative default)

  consistency_weight:
    Based on CONS_001–CONS_006 signals over 30-day window:
    < 3 sessions/week:  0.70
    3–5 sessions/week:  0.90
    5–7 sessions/week:  1.00
    Daily + streaks:    1.10 (cap — prevents burnout gaming)

  integrity_weight:
    Based on COPY_PROBABILITY_AGENT + ACCESS_LOG_TRACKER signals:
    No anomaly flags:               1.00
    LOW anomaly flag:               0.90
    MEDIUM anomaly flag:            0.70
    HIGH anomaly flag:              0.40
    CRITICAL anomaly flag:          0.00 (credit suspended pending review)

  novelty_weight:
    Rewards improvement on NEW skills vs. same repeated scenario:
    First 3 attempts on a scenario: 1.00
    Attempts 4–10:                  0.80
    Attempts 11–20:                 0.60
    Attempts 21+:                   0.30 (steep diminishing return)
    New scenario never attempted:   1.20 (first-mover novelty bonus)
```

### 4.3 — Weak Area Multiplier

```
If the improvement signal is specifically in a flagged WEAK AREA
(as identified by the Weakness Detection Algorithm):

  weak_area_multiplier = 1.50

This multiplier applies ONLY when:
  — The improvement is in a weak area identified at least 14 days ago
  — The weak area score delta is ≥ +10 percentage points
  — The improvement is confirmed over at least 3 sessions

Rationale: Closing a genuine weakness is the most valuable
improvement on the platform and must be visibly rewarded.
```

### 4.4 — Improvement Score (Composite)

```
IMPROVEMENT_SCORE (0.00–100.00) is computed as a WEIGHTED COMPOSITE
of all active signals in the measurement window:

  improvement_score =
    Σ (signal_weight[i] × quality_adjusted_delta[i] × normalisation_factor)

SIGNAL WEIGHTS (% of total score):
  TIER 1 — PRIMARY PERFORMANCE     55% total
    PERF_001 dojo_score_delta:      18%
    PERF_002 gd_score_delta:        15%
    PERF_003 assessment_delta:      10%
    PERF_004 project_ppr_delta:      8%
    PERF_005 elo_delta:              4% (supporting signal)

  TIER 2 — SKILL DEPTH             25% total
    SKILL_001 weak_area_recovery:   12% (highest individual weight in tier)
    SKILL_002 unlock_velocity:       5%
    SKILL_003 difficulty_progression: 5%
    SKILL_004 cross_domain:          3%

  TIER 3 — CONSISTENCY             12% total
    CONS_001 streak:                 5%
    CONS_002 practice_frequency:     4%
    CONS_005 completion_rate:        3%

  TIER 4 — SOCIAL LEARNING          5% total
    SOCIAL_001 peer_review_uptake:   3%
    SOCIAL_002 study_partner:        2%

  TIER 5 — CREATOR (creators only)  3% additional (does not reduce Tier 1–4)
    CREATE_001–004 signals:          1% each

NORMALISATION:
  Scores normalised to 0–100 range per signal
  using max observed delta in cohort over 90-day rolling window
  Zero floor: no signal contributes negative to improvement_score
  (regression is tracked separately as a regression flag)
```

### 4.5 — Improvement Tier Classification

```
IMPROVEMENT_TIER mapped from improvement_score:

  0.00–19.99    → STAGNANT
                   No meaningful growth detected.
                   Flag: stagnation_alert emitted to NOTIFICATION_AGENT

  20.00–39.99   → DEVELOPING
                   Early growth indicators present.
                   Credit: base rate, no bonus multiplier

  40.00–59.99   → PROGRESSING
                   Consistent measurable improvement across signals.
                   Credit: 1.1× bonus multiplier applied

  60.00–79.99   → ACCELERATING
                   Strong multi-signal improvement with consistency.
                   Credit: 1.25× bonus multiplier applied

  80.00–100.00  → ELITE

## 7️⃣ ANTI-GAMING CATALOGUE (COMPLETE REGISTRY)

Every detected gaming pattern has a registered detection code:

```
VOLUME GAMING:
  GAME_VOL_001 — Rapid repetition: same scenario attempted > 10× in 24h
  GAME_VOL_002 — Easy-only pattern: > 80% attempts on EASY tier over 30 days
  GAME_VOL_003 — Session volume spike: session count > 4σ above 30-day baseline
  GAME_VOL_004 — Diminishing delta: score plateau after 15+ repetitions
                 (score improvement < 2% across 15 attempts = zero credit)

STAT PADDING:
  GAME_PAD_001 — Score ceiling repetition: repeating scenario already scored > 90%
  GAME_PAD_002 — Selective domain gaming: avoiding all scenarios in flagged weak areas
  GAME_PAD_003 — Belt threshold gaming: precisely hitting belt score criteria,
                 then stopping (no further activity within 7 days of belt award)

BUDDY BOOSTING:
  GAME_BOOS_001 — Mutual win trading: user A beats user B, user B beats user A,
                  repeat pattern ≥ 4× in 7 days with no third participants
  GAME_BOOS_002 — Rank-isolated cluster: ≥ 3 actors in mutual practice cluster
                  with no outside matches, all gaining ELO simultaneously
  GAME_BOOS_003 — Timing correlation: sessions between two actors scheduled
                  within 5 minutes of each other, consistently, ≥ 5× in 7 days

ANOMALOUS VELOCITY:
  GAME_VEL_001 — Score jump: single-session score improvement > +30 points
                 with no prior gradual improvement signals (overnight jump)
  GAME_VEL_002 — ELO spike: ELO gain > 200 in 48 hours (statistically improbable)
  GAME_VEL_003 — Weak area disappearance: flagged weak area score improves > +25
                 in a single session (no supporting practice history)

SESSION INTEGRITY:
  GAME_INT_001 — Session abandonment gaming: abandoning sessions when score
                 trajectory is negative, then restarting (cherry-picking)
  GAME_INT_002 — Hint abuse: hint usage rate > 3× personal baseline (gaming
                 hint penalty system for net-positive hint profit)
  GAME_INT_003 — Keyboard/behavioural anomaly: paste-heavy session correlated
                 with suspiciously high score (cross-signal with BEHAVIOUR_TRACKING)
  GAME_INT_004 — Time anomaly: session completed significantly faster than
                 scenario minimum expected completion time

CREDIT GAMING:
  GAME_CRED_001 — Stagnation disguise: artificially maintaining DEVELOPING tier
                  to avoid STAGNANT detection while earning base credits slowly
  GAME_CRED_002 — Multiplier farming: timing activity bursts precisely at
                  consistency_weight maximum windows
  GAME_CRED_003 — Weak area fake closure: scoring well once in weak area
                  then returning to avoidance (one-session credit grab)

GAMING RESPONSE POLICY:
  LOW (1–2 signals, low severity):
    → credit_action = REDUCED (integrity_weight = 0.70)
    → actor warned via NOTIFICATION_AGENT
    → monitoring period: 14 days
