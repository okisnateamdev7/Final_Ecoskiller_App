## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ❌ Don't:
- Make claims without data backing
- Use vague language ("improve" vs "34% improvement")
- Ignore parent audience
- Focus only on winners (address struggling students too)
- Change messaging too frequently
- Promise unrealistic outcomes
- Ignore platform-specific best practices
- Forget to test before scaling

### ✓ Do:
- Lead with numbers and proof
- Test messaging with target audience first
- Involve parents in the narrative
- Tell diverse stories (struggle to success)
- Be consistent in core messaging
- Under-promise, over-deliver
- Optimize for each platform
- A/B test continuously
