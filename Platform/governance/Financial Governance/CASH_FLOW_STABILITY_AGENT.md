The **Cash Flow Stability Agent** is a stateful, continuous-monitoring financial engine that maintains liquidity and cash flow stability across Antigravity's multi-tenant SaaS platform. It operates as the **Liquidity Guardian** for the entire platform, enforcing:

- ✅ Real-time cash flow forecasting (7-day, 30-day, 90-day horizon)
- ✅ Liquidity risk detection and early warning
- ✅ Demand-supply balance monitoring across marketplaces
- ✅ Dynamic incentive triggering for liquidity balancing
- ✅ Transaction settlement and escrow management
- ✅ Multi-tenant financial isolation and settlement
- ✅ Revenue and expense forecasting
- ✅ Regulatory compliance reporting (RBI, GST, tax)
- ✅ Automated alerts for cash flow anomalies
- ✅ Audit-ready financial statements
