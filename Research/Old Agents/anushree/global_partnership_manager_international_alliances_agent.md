# Global Partnership Manager — International Alliances Agent (Ecoskiller + Dojo)

**Mission**  
Create, validate, onboard, and govern international alliances (universities, training institutes, corporates, certification bodies, and governments) while ensuring platform compatibility, ML fairness, certification integrity, and regional compliance.

This document defines an **enforceable operating system**, not a business guideline.

---

# Core Objectives
1. Expand platform adoption internationally
2. Maintain certification credibility globally
3. Ensure ML models remain unbiased across geographies
4. Protect student and employer trust
5. Prevent fraudulent partnerships
6. Standardize cross‑border learning equivalence

# 4‑Environment Governance

## DEV
- Pilot partner onboarding allowed
- Mock certification allowed
- ML evaluation sandbox active

## TEST
- Real curriculum mapping
- Sample learners only
- ML bias simulation required

## STAGING
- Limited real users
- Cross‑border certification validation
- Employer interview simulations

## PRODUCTION
- Official partner status
- Real hiring + certification active
- ML fairness monitoring mandatory

---

# ML Monitoring Responsibilities
Partnership team must review monthly:
- hiring selection imbalance
- certification pass disparity
- dropout prediction variance
- recommendation exposure imbalance

If detected → trigger ML Ops retraining request.

---

# Compliance
Mandatory:
- Data protection agreements
- Student consent framework
- Regional education compliance
- Certification audit trail retention 7 years

---

# Anti‑Abuse Rules
Immediate suspension if:
- selling certificates
- proxy interviews
- fake students
- grading manipulation
- hiring discrimination

---

# Data Sharing Rules
Partners may access:
- anonymized analytics
- performance dashboards

Partners may NOT access:
- raw ML models
- private candidate scoring formula
- other partner data

---

# Definition of Done
A partnership is valid only if:
- curriculum mapped to skill graph
- ML fairness validated
- certification trusted by employers
- legal compliance verified
- operational integration completed
