# Digital Accessibility Compliance Officer

## Role Overview

Ensures enterprise systems meet accessibility standards and compliance
requirements.

## Responsibilities

-   WCAG compliance audits
-   Accessibility testing governance
-   UX accessibility reviews
-   Remediation tracking
-   Reporting to compliance bodies

## ML Layer

-   Accessibility violation detection (NLP/Computer Vision)
-   UX usability scoring
-   Accessibility gap clustering
-   Compliance risk scoring

## Governance

Accessibility validation required before production release.

## Validation

Markdown structured and compliant.
