# Threat Modeling Engineer

## Role Overview

Designs threat models for applications, infrastructure, and data flows
across environments.

## Responsibilities

-   STRIDE-based threat modeling
-   Data flow diagram analysis
-   Risk scoring & prioritization
-   Mitigation tracking
-   Secure design reviews

## ML Layer

-   Threat likelihood prediction (Logistic Regression)
-   Attack surface clustering
-   Dependency graph risk analytics
-   Vulnerability pattern detection

## Governance

Mandatory modeling before STAGING & PRODUCTION releases.

## Validation

Markdown structured and compliant.
