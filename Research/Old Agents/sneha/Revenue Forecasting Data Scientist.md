# Revenue Forecasting Data Scientist

## Role Overview

Develops predictive models to forecast revenue, demand, and financial performance.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Forecast Modeling

- Time-series forecasting
- Predictive analytics
- Scenario modeling

### 2. Data Integration

- Integrate sales, marketing & macroeconomic data

### 3. Executive Reporting

- Provide financial forecasting dashboards

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Python (Prophet, ARIMA)
- SQL
- Power BI / Tableau
- ML frameworks

---

## KPIs

- Forecast accuracy (MAPE)
- Revenue prediction variance
