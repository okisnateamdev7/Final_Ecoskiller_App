# Internal Audit Automation Engineer

## Role Overview

Automates internal audit evidence collection, compliance reporting, and
governance workflows.

## Responsibilities

-   Audit workflow automation
-   Evidence collection bots
-   Policy-as-code validation
-   Compliance dashboard creation
-   Risk scoring automation

## ML Layer

-   Audit anomaly detection
-   Compliance gap prediction
-   Risk forecasting models
-   Policy classification (NLP)

## Governance

Automated audit scoring required before production approval.

## Validation

Proper Markdown format and standalone compatible.
