# Behavioral Economics Analyst

## Role Overview

Applies behavioral science principles to optimize customer decision-making, engagement, and conversion strategies.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Experiment Design

- Design A/B testing frameworks
- Identify behavioral biases in funnels

### 2. Behavioral Modeling

- Apply nudging strategies
- Analyze cognitive decision patterns

### 3. Revenue & Growth Impact

- Optimize pricing presentation
- Improve user retention mechanics

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Experimentation platforms
- Python / R
- Statistical modeling tools

---

## KPIs

- Conversion uplift %
- Retention improvement
- Experiment win rate
