# Institutional Reporting Engineer

## Role Overview

Designs automated reporting systems for institutional stakeholders including investors and regulators.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Reporting Automation

- Build data pipelines for reporting
- Ensure financial reporting accuracy

### 2. Data Validation

- Implement reconciliation controls

### 3. Stakeholder Delivery

- Maintain reporting SLAs
- Support audit processes

---

## Tools & Technologies

- SQL
- ETL tools
- Financial reporting systems

---

## KPIs

- Report accuracy
- On-time delivery %
