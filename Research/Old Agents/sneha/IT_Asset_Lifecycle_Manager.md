# IT Asset Lifecycle Manager --- Asset Inventory

## Role Overview

Manages procurement, tracking, compliance, lifecycle management, and
decommissioning of IT assets.

## Responsibilities

-   Asset inventory tracking
-   Hardware lifecycle governance
-   Procurement workflows
-   Warranty & compliance monitoring
-   Disposal & secure wipe management
-   License-to-asset mapping

## ML Layer

-   Asset depreciation forecasting (Time-Series)
-   Asset risk scoring (Logistic Regression)
-   Inventory anomaly detection (Isolation Forest)
-   Lifecycle optimization prediction

## Governance

Full visibility across DEV, TEST, STAGING, PRODUCTION environments.

## Validation

Proper Markdown structure, Git-ready.
