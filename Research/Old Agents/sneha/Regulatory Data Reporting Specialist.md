# Regulatory Data Reporting Specialist

## Role Overview

Ensures regulatory compliance through accurate and timely submission of required data to governing authorities.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Regulatory Compliance

- Prepare regulatory filings
- Monitor regulation updates

### 2. Data Validation

- Ensure compliance accuracy
- Maintain audit trails

### 3. Governance Coordination

- Work with legal & compliance teams

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Regulatory reporting systems
- SQL
- Data validation tools

---

## KPIs

- Regulatory compliance rate
- Audit findings count
