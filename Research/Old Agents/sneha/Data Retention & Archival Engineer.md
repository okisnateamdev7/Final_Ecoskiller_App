# Data Retention & Archival Engineer

## Role Overview

Manages enterprise data lifecycle including retention policies, archival strategies, and deletion compliance.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Retention Policy Implementation

- Define retention timelines
- Automate deletion workflows

### 2. Archival Systems

- Design cold storage solutions
- Optimize storage costs

### 3. Compliance & Legal Support

- Support data subject requests
- Ensure regulatory adherence

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Cloud archival storage (S3 Glacier)
- Backup systems
- Lifecycle management tools

---

## KPIs

- Storage cost optimization
- Compliance audit success
