# Master Data Management (MDM) Specialist

## Role Overview

Responsible for defining and governing core enterprise master data such as customers, products, and vendors.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Master Data Strategy

- Define golden record models
- Establish survivorship rules

### 2. Governance & Stewardship

- Maintain data ownership model
- Manage deduplication processes

### 3. System Integration

- Synchronize master data across systems
- Ensure consistency across ERP & CRM

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Informatica MDM
- SAP MDG
- Oracle MDM
- Data quality tools

---

## KPIs

- Duplicate reduction rate
- Master record accuracy
