# Knowledge Management Platform Owner --- Internal Wiki & Documentation Systems

## Role Overview

Owns internal documentation, wiki systems, knowledge indexing, and
collaboration platforms.

## Responsibilities

-   Wiki governance
-   Documentation lifecycle management
-   Access controls
-   Search optimization
-   Knowledge indexing

## ML Layer

-   NLP document classification
-   Semantic search (Transformer models)
-   Knowledge gap detection
-   Content recommendation engine

## Governance

Version control, approval workflows, audit logging.

## Validation

Proper Markdown formatted document.
