# 3. Platform Monetization Governance Across Environments

Strict environment separation is required for revenue simulations and testing.

---

## 3.1 Development (DEV)

**Purpose**

- Prototype monetization workflows
- Test pricing model algorithms
- Simulate revenue scenarios

**Rules**

- Synthetic financial & usage data only
- No live billing impact
- Debug-level logging for simulations
- Early-stage AI revenue modeling
- Workflow validation required before TEST promotion

---

## 3.2 Testing (TEST)

**Purpose**

- Validate pricing & subscription workflows
- Test revenue tracking & reporting systems

**Rules**

- Simulate monetization scenarios using test data
- Validate revenue calculation logic
- KPI measurement for monetization effectiveness
- Logging level: INFO
- Multi-team review for validation

---

## 3.3 Staging (PRE-PRODUCTION)

**Purpose**

- Production-like monetization & revenue simulation
- Executive review of dashboards & models

**Rules**

- Use anonymized production usage & revenue data
- End-to-end workflow validation
- Compliance & audit verification
- Logging level: WARN
- Executive review required

---

## 3.4 Production (PROD)

**Purpose**

- Live revenue optimization & monetization

**Rules**

- Real-time pricing, subscription, and usage tracking
- Automated revenue reporting
- AI-driven revenue forecasting active
- KPI dashboards for executives
- Logging level: ERROR
- Audit-ready financial logs
