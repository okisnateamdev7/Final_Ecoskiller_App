# Community Experience Designer — Complete Gap Register

Status: ADD-ONLY · AUDIT-READY · GOVERNANCE PATCH SET
Scope: Ecoskiller + Dojo Community Systems

This document consolidates **ALL IDENTIFIED GAPS (1–25)** discovered across multiple hard rechecks.
Each gap is designable, enforceable, auditable, and scoped strictly to the Community Experience domain.

---

## GAP 1 — COMMUNITY ↔ SKILL / DOJO CONTEXT ISOLATION

**Problem**
Community discussions can be misinterpreted as evaluative signals.

**Risk**
Assessment integrity contamination.

**Mandatory Rule**
- Community interactions must be explicitly tagged as non-evaluative.
- No community signal may influence scoring, belts, ranks, or certification.

---

## GAP 2 — COMMUNITY ↔ CERTIFICATION INFERENCE

**Problem**
Popularity may imply false expertise.

**Mandatory Rule**
- Community reputation must never appear near certifications.
- Explicit disclaimer required.

---

## GAP 3 — INSTITUTION / COMPANY POWER ASYMMETRY

**Problem**
Organizations may silence weaker participants.

**Mandatory Rule**
- Org admins forbidden from moderating anonymous complaint channels.
- Platform governance overrides org moderation.

---

## GAP 4 — ANONYMOUS COMPLAINT RETALIATION PROTECTION

**Problem**
Post-complaint retaliation risk.

**Mandatory Rule**
- Retaliation ML detection.
- Automatic escalation on patterns.

---

## GAP 5 — COMMUNITY LEGAL HOLD

**Problem**
Evidence deletion during disputes.

**Mandatory Rule**
- Legal-hold state freezes deletion until resolution.

---

## GAP 6 — CROSS-TENANT COMMUNITY LEAKAGE

**Problem**
Content escaping tenant boundary.

**Mandatory Rule**
- Tenant-scoped feeds, search, mentions.

---

## GAP 7 — COMMUNITY SEARCH RANKING GOVERNANCE

**Problem**
Popularity bias.

**Mandatory Rule**
- Relevance-first ranking.
- Authority caps.

---

## GAP 8 — MODERATOR BIAS & DRIFT

**Problem**
Silent over-moderation.

- Periodic re-certification.

## GAP 9 — COMMUNITY INCIDENT MODE

**Problem**
Undefined behavior during outages.
