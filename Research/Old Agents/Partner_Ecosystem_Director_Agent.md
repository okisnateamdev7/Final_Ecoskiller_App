# 3. Partner Ecosystem Governance Across Environments

Strict environment separation is required for partner simulations and performance testing.

---

## 3.1 Development (DEV)

**Purpose**

- Prototype partner workflows
- Simulate partner engagement scenarios
- Develop onboarding & compliance templates

**Rules**

- Synthetic partner data only
- No live partner communication
- Debug-level logging for simulations
- Early-stage AI partner analytics sandboxed
- Workflow validation required before TEST promotion

---

## 3.2 Testing (TEST)

**Purpose**

- Validate partner onboarding workflows
- Test compliance & engagement procedures
- Validate AI-driven analytics

**Rules**

- Simulate engagement & performance metrics
- Compliance rule verification
- KPI measurement for partner onboarding
- Logging level: INFO
- Multi-team review for validation

---

## 3.3 Staging (PRE-PRODUCTION)

**Purpose**

- Production-like partner engagement simulation
- Executive review of onboarding & performance dashboards

**Rules**

- Use anonymized production partner data
- End-to-end engagement workflow testing
- Regulatory and contractual compliance verification
- Logging level: WARN
- Executive review required

---

## 3.4 Production (PROD)

**Purpose**

- Live partner ecosystem operations & engagement

**Rules**

- Real-time partner onboarding and performance tracking
- Automated compliance verification
- Revenue & operational alignment monitoring
- KPI dashboards for executives
- Logging level: ERROR
- Audit-ready governance logs

# 10. Enforcement Policy

If any system violates:

- Partner onboarding compliance rules
- Contractual obligations
- Engagement workflows

The PED Agent must:

1. Freeze partner access or workflows
2. Trigger internal audit and review
3. Initiate remediation or improvement workflow
4. Escalate to CRO/CEO if unresolved
