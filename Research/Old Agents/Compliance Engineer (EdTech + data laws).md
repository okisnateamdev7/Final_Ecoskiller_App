# GAP 1

## 1. Description

Structural or regulatory compliance weakness identified within the
Ecoskiller + Dojo ecosystem.

## 2. Legal / Regulatory Risk

Potential exposure includes: - Administrative fines - AI Act conformity
violation - Educational data misuse claims - Anti-discrimination
liability - Contract breach exposure - Regulatory enforcement
escalation - Cross-border transfer violation - Audit failure

## 3. Required Technical Controls

-   Enforce lawful basis validation at API layer
-   Maintain controller/processor role mapping
-   Apply region-based policy enforcement
-   Restrict automated decision impact where required
-   Ensure immutable audit logging
-   Bind AI model versions to compliance documentation
-   Implement cross-border validation checks
-   Maintain strict data minimization & retention policies
-   Enforce environment isolation (DEV/TEST/STAGING/PROD)
-   Ensure no compliance-critical feature flag can disable controls

## 4. Required Governance Controls

-   Assign control owner with RACI mapping
-   Perform DPIA or AIA where applicable
-   Record risk assessment & mitigation plan
-   Obtain board-level review for high-risk AI
-   Maintain vendor & subprocessor registry
-   Conduct annual regulatory review
-   Enforce independent compliance testing
-   Maintain override logging & TTL enforcement

## 5. ML / AI Oversight Controls (If Applicable)

-   Bias & fairness metric monitoring
-   Drift detection for compliance-sensitive outputs
-   Model risk reclassification after retraining
-   Human appeal workflow logging
-   AI incident reporting mechanism

-   Training data license validation

Structural or regulatory compliance weakness identified within the
Ecoskiller + Dojo ecosystem.

## 2. Legal / Regulatory Risk

Potential exposure includes: - Administrative fines - AI Act conformity
violation - Educational data misuse claims - Anti-discrimination
liability - Contract breach exposure - Regulatory enforcement
escalation - Cross-border transfer violation - Audit failure

## 3. Required Technical Controls

-   Enforce lawful basis validation at API layer
-   Maintain controller/processor role mapping
-   Apply region-based policy enforcement
-   Restrict automated decision impact where required
-   Ensure immutable audit logging
-   Bind AI model versions to compliance documentation
-   Implement cross-border validation checks
-   Maintain strict data minimization & retention policies
-   Enforce environment isolation (DEV/TEST/STAGING/PROD)
-   Ensure no compliance-critical feature flag can disable controls

## 4. Required Governance Controls

-   Assign control owner with RACI mapping
-   Perform DPIA or AIA where applicable
-   Record risk assessment & mitigation plan
-   Obtain board-level review for high-risk AI
-   Maintain vendor & subprocessor registry
-   Conduct annual regulatory review
-   Enforce independent compliance testing
-   Maintain override logging & TTL enforcement

## 5. ML / AI Oversight Controls (If Applicable)

-   Bias & fairness metric monitoring
-   Drift detection for compliance-sensitive outputs
-   Model risk reclassification after retraining
-   Human appeal workflow logging
-   AI incident reporting mechanism
-   Dataset lawful basis verification
-   Training data license validation

## 6. Audit Evidence Requirements
