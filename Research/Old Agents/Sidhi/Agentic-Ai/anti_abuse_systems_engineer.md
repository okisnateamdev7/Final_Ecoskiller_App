# ANTI-ABUSE SYSTEMS ENGINEER — LAYER 10
## ABUSE PREVENTION & DETECTION · ECOSKILLER CORE

---

## 1. MISSION

Design automated systems to detect, prevent, and mitigate abuse before community harm occurs.

---

## 2. ML MODELS REQUIRED

- Bot detection (Random Forest)
- Sybil attack detection
- Rate-limit anomaly detection
- Graph-based abuse clustering
- Temporal behavior modeling

---

## 3. ABUSE SIGNALS

Tracked:
- Posting velocity
- Similarity fingerprints
- IP entropy
- Device graph correlation

---

## 4. AUTOMATED RESPONSE TIERS

Tier 1: Throttle  
Tier 2: Shadow ban  
Tier 3: Lock + Review  
Tier 4: Permanent ban (human approval)

---

## 5. ENVIRONMENT CONTROLS

Production models must be:
- Versioned
- Rollback-safe
- Bias-audited
